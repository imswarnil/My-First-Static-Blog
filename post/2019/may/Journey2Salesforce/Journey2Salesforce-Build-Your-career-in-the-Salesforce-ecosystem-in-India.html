This is a great opportunity Provided by salesforce for all the residents of india.Where you can learn Salesforce skills and you can build your career in the Salesforce. Journey to Salesforce is a 3+ month learning program by salesforce, where they will send you study material which you have to study in 2 weeks after completing one module they will send you 6 modules which is Trailmix that you to complete in 3 months after completing trailmix modules that teach you everything from the fundamentals of the Salesforce platform, to the automation, security, and custom coding. How You Can Start Journey2Salesforce ?

  • Register for #Journey2Salesforce here Registration Link Sign Up Button will be there fill all the details and sign up.
